Welcome

Welcome to Rank & File 6787 — an independent member space built around union solidarity, open discussion, shared information, and rank-and-file power.

This site exists to give working people a place to stay informed, raise concerns, share ideas, and speak honestly without leadership gatekeeping.

About This Space

Rank & File 6787 is built on the belief that strong unions come from informed members, active participation, and open communication from the bottom up.

The goal is simple: support worker-to-worker communication, preserve free speech, and encourage rank-and-file control over the direction, priorities, and future of the union.

Whether members are sharing updates, asking questions, discussing workplace issues, or building solidarity, this space is here to serve the membership.
